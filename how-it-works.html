How Operion Works

Operion receives business enquiries from multiple channels and processes them using a controlled AI pipeline:

  1. Multi-channel intake (Web, Email, API)
  2. Secure queue storage (Neon DB)
  3. Deterministic processing with audit logging
  4. AI execution (Ollama + fallback)
  5. Structured response delivery

Security & Reliability

  • Input validation & sanitisation
  • Rate limiting & abuse detection
  • Audit logging & failover protection
  • Controlled AI execution with fallback models
